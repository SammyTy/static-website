Benefits of the Galvalnize Tech
Community

The Galvanize Tech Community (GTC) is the online community to connect with aspiring
and professional software engineers and data scientists (like you!), access valuable tech
resources, learn about upcoming events in the industry, and advance your career skills
all in one, inclusive place.

Meet others in the Tech community

Whether you're learning how to code for the first time or a professional in the tech
industry, there's a place for you to connect through #women, and LGBTQIA, cities
throughout the US, and many more channels. Develop your network of other engineers
and data scientists to motivate each other and celebrate #wins.

Access valuable resorces, content, Conservation and support.

Gain access to our vault of content around hot industry topics (like Data Visualization,
Cybersecurity, and more), helpful resources from other members, and discussions around
current tech industry events. If you're learning how to code, the
#sei-prep channel is the space to ask for peer-to-peer help on coding problems and
receive support during the weekly study halls.

Gain access to learning and networking opportunities

Members are the first to know about discounted and exclusive tickets to industry
conferences, technical talks from industry leaders, premium content from community
partners like TigerGraph, Data Science Salon, and DeveloperWeek.

Be the first to know about Galvalnize Community and Workshop

Galvanize hosts 40+ events per month, ranging from technical workshops, like beginner
JavaScript courses, to soft skills workshops, like Dealing with Imposter Syndrome. Our >
weekly roundup of announcements and events make it convenient to sign up for the
events that speak to you!
